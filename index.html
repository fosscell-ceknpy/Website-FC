About Us

FOSS cell CEKNPY is a student organization at the College of Engineering Karunagappally (CEKNPY) dedicated to promoting and contributing to Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).
The cell was founded in 2022 by a group of students who were passionate about FOSS and wanted to create a community where they could learn, collaborate, and contribute to open source projects.

  • Organizing talks and workshops on FOSS
  • Hosting hackathons and other events
  • Contributing to open source projects

The cell is supported by ICFOSS, the Kerala chapter of the Free Software Foundation India. ICFOSS provides the cell with resources and guidance, and helps the cell to connect with other FOSS communities in Kerala.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is FOSS?

    FOSS stands for Free and Open Source Software. It is software that is developed and distributed with its source code freely available. This means that anyone can use, modify, and redistribute the software without any restrictions.

  • Why should I join FOSS cell CEKNPY?

    There are many benefits to joining FOSS cell CEKNPY. Here are a few:
    * You can learn about FOSS and how it works.
    * You can collaborate with other developers on open source projects.
    * You can gain experience in open source development.
    * You can network with other FOSS enthusiasts.
    * You can get involved in the FOSS community.

  • What are the activities of FOSS cell CEKNPY?

    FOSS cell CEKNPY organizes a variety of activities to promote and contribute to FOSS. These activities include:
    * Talks and workshops on FOSS
    * Hackathons
    * Mentorship programs
    * Contributions to open source projects

  • What are the requirements to join FOSS cell CEKNPY?

    There are no formal requirements to join FOSS cell CEKNPY. However, you should be interested in learning about FOSS and open source development.

  • What are the benefits of contributing to open source projects?

    There are many benefits to contributing to open source projects. Here are a few:
    * You can learn new skills and techniques.
    * You can get your work seen by a large audience.
    * You can collaborate with other developers from all over the world.
    * You can make a difference in the world.
